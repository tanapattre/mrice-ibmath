IB DP Mathematics: AI SL — Probability & Statistics · Syllabus 4.7

🔑 Recall — Probability Distributions

  • Probability distribution table: Lists all possible values of $X$ with their probabilities $P(X = x)$
  • Properties: $0 \leq P(X = x) \leq 1$ and $\sum P(X = x) = 1$
  • Expected value: $\displaystyle \E(X) = \sum x \cdot P(X = x)$
  • For combined events: list all outcomes from the sample space first, then group by final score

📘 Worked Example

A game is played by tossing a fair coin and rolling a fair six-sided die.

  • If the coin shows tails, the score on the die is the final score.
  • If the coin shows heads, 2 is added to the score on the die.

Step 1 — List all outcomes:

H1, H2, H3, H4, H5, H6, T1, T2, T3, T4, T5, T6

Step 2 — Map to final scores:

OutcomeH1H2H3H4H5H6T1T2T3T4T5T6
Score345678123456

Step 3 — Build the probability distribution table:

Score $x$12345678
$P(X = x)$$\frac{1}{12}$$\frac{1}{12}$$\frac{2}{12}$$\frac{2}{12}$$\frac{2}{12}$$\frac{2}{12}$$\frac{1}{12}$$\frac{1}{12}$

Check: $\frac{1+1+2+2+2+2+1+1}{12} = 1$ ✓

Note: Scores 3, 4, 5, 6 each have two paths (e.g., score 3 = H1 or T3)

Step 4 — Calculate expected value:

$$\E(X) = 1 \times \frac{1}{12} + 2 \times \frac{1}{12} + 3 \times \frac{2}{12} + 4 \times \frac{2}{12} + 5 \times \frac{2}{12} + 6 \times \frac{2}{12} + 7 \times \frac{1}{12} + 8 \times \frac{1}{12}$$ $$\E(X) = \frac{1 + 2 + 6 + 8 + 10 + 12 + 7 + 8}{12} = \frac{54}{12} = 4.5$$

A fair coin is tossed. If it shows heads, the score is $2 \times$ (the number on a rolled die). If it shows tails, the score is just the number on the die.

(a) Complete the probability distribution table for the final score. (3 marks)

Question 2 — Medium[5 marks]

A spinner has values 1, 2, 3, 4 with equal probability. A fair coin is tossed.

  • If heads: final score = spinner value
  • If tails: final score = spinner value + 2

(a) Complete the probability distribution table for the final score. (3 marks)

(b) Find $\P(X = 5)$. (1 mark)

(c) Calculate $\E(X)$. (1 mark)

Question 3 — Exam Style[6 marks]

Two fair dice are rolled.

  • If the sum is even: score = sum
  • If the sum is odd: score = sum + 3

(a) Complete the probability distribution table showing possible scores and their probabilities. (3 marks)

(b) Find $\P(X = 9)$. (1 mark)

(c) Calculate $\E(X)$. (2 marks)

Question 4 — Challenging[5 marks]

A game costs \$2 to play. A fair die is rolled and the player wins $\$ \times$ (the number on the die).

(a) Let $Y$ be the net profit. Complete the probability distribution table for $Y$. (2 marks)

(b) Calculate $\E(Y)$. (2 marks)

(c) Determine whether the game is fair. Justify your answer. (1 mark)
