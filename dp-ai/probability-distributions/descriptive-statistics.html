🔑 Recall — Descriptive Statistics

  • Mode: The most frequently occurring value.
  • Median: The middle value when data is ordered. For even $n$, average the two middle values.
  • Mean: $\displaystyle \bar{x} = \frac{\sum x}{n}$
  • Range: Maximum value minus minimum value.
  • Standard deviation ($\sigma$): Use your GDC (1-variable statistics). For IB AI SL, use population SD.

📘 Worked Example

Find the descriptive statistics for the following data set:

14, 17, 20, 17, 15, 11, 14, 20, 20, 29

Step 1 — Order the data:

11, 14, 14, 15, 17, 17, 20, 20, 20, 29

Step 2 — Mode:

20 appears 3 times (most frequent)

Step 3 — Median ($n = 10$, even):

Middle two values are 17 and 17 → Median = $\frac{17 + 17}{2} = $ 17

Step 4 — Mean:

$\bar{x} = \frac{11 + 14 + 14 + 15 + 17 + 17 + 20 + 20 + 20 + 29}{10} = \frac{177}{10} = $ 17.7

Step 5 — Range:

Range = $29 - 11 = $ 18

Step 6 — Standard deviation (GDC):

Enter data into STAT → EDIT, then 1-VAR STATS → $\sigma_x = $ 5.12 (3 sf)

Question 1 — Basic[4 marks]

Consider the following data:

8, 12, 15, 12, 10, 18, 15, 15, 22

(a) Find the mode. (1 mark)

(b) Find the median. (1 mark)

(c) Calculate the mean. (1 mark)

(d) Find the range. (1 mark)

Question 2 — Medium[5 marks]

Given the data:

5, 8, 10, 12, 12, 15, 18, 20, 22, 25, 28, 30

(a) Find the mode, median, mean, and range. (2 marks)

(b) Calculate the standard deviation using your GDC. (1 mark)

(c) The value 50 is added to the data set. Calculate the new mean, median, and standard deviation. (2 marks)

Question 3 — Exam Style[4 marks]

Consider the following list of 10 data items:

16, 19, 21, 19, 18, 12, 16, 21, 21, 32

The mean of this data is 19.5.

(a) Find the value of the mode. (1 mark)

(b) Find the value of the median. (1 mark)

(c) Calculate the standard deviation. (1 mark)

(d) Find the range. (1 mark)

Question 4 — Challenging[5 marks]

Two data sets are given:

Set A: 10, 12, 15, 18, 20, 22, 25     Set B: 14, 15, 16, 17, 18, 19, 20

(a) For each set, calculate the mean, median, standard deviation, and range. (3 marks)

(b) Compare the two data sets, referring to mean, median, standard deviation, and range. (1 mark)

(c) Determine which data set is more consistent and which has the higher central tendency. (1 mark)

Question 1 — Basic [4 marks]

(a) Mode = 15 A1
(b) Ordered: 8, 10, 12, 12, 15, 15, 15, 18, 22 → Median = 15 A1
(c) Mean = $\frac{8+12+15+12+10+18+15+15+22}{9} = \frac{127}{9} = $ 14.1 (3 sf) A1
(d) Range = $22 - 8 = $ 14 A1

Question 2 — Medium [5 marks]

(a) Mode = 12; Median = $\frac{15+18}{2} = $ 16.5; Mean = $\frac{225}{12} = $ 18.75; Range = $30-5 = $ 25 A1A1
(b) SD = 7.75 (3 sf) A1
(c) New mean = $\frac{275}{13} = $ 21.2; New median = 18; New SD = 11.6 M1A1

(a) Mode = 21 A1
(b) Ordered: 12, 16, 16, 18, 19, 19, 21, 21, 21, 32 → Median = $\frac{19+19}{2} = $ 19 A1
