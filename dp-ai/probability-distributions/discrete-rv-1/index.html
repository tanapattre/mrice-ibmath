Discrete Random Variables 1

IB DP Mathematics: AI SL · Syllabus 4.7

📊 Lesson Overview

This lesson introduces discrete random variables, probability distributions, and expected value.

  • Probability distributions — listing all possible values with their probabilities
  • Expected value E(X) — the mean outcome over many trials
  • "Number of phones" case study — real-world application
  • Examples from Fannon, Agresti & Franklin, and Wazir et al. textbooks
