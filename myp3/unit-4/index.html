📖 Study Guide

The study guide covers key formulas, terms, and concepts for the Unit 4 probability test.

✏️ Unit 4 Test

A Criteria C & D test with a Rock Paper Scissors theme. Students work through sample spaces, tree diagrams, and probability calculations in context.

🔍 Beyond the Unit Investigations

Five enrichment activities for Criterion B investigation:

  • Birthday Paradox — Probability of shared birthdays in a group
  • Monty Hall Problem — Conditional probability in game shows
  • Bertrand's Box Paradox — Classical probability paradox
  • Gambler's Fallacy — Misunderstanding of independent events
  • Pascal's Triangle & Combinatorics — Patterns and counting
