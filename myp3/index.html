MYP3 Enrichment

For students who finish class work early

Theme: Using probability to help us when we play games.

These worksheets explore counting principles, probability, and game strategy through board games, dice games, and card games. Work through them independently when you finish your regular class work.

Vocabulary, basic probability, complementary events, sample spaces, 2D plots, tree diagrams (1 and 2 events), independent/dependent events, with/without replacement. Scrambled order — no sections!
